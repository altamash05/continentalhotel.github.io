Welcome to the Continental

Welcome to the Hotel Continental, the most private and biggest of all Delhi hotels . This haven of greenery that used to belong to the Oberoi family and became a Private House allows you to discover the largest hotel garden in the capital., Over time this family house that was once called “The Cottage” has established itself as a reference in New Delhi Whether for a drink, for a dinner or for a night, the Hotel Continental awaits you in Delhi in what many people rightly call “a countryside in the heart of Delhi”. A Private House full of life, especially for epicureans of the night, who find refuge there in its hidden den: its cocktail bar Particulier.
